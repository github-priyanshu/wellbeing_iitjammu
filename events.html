Celebrating International Day of Yoga

IIT Jammu organised a community Yoga session to mark the International Day of Yoga. Students and staff participated in guided asanas and mindfulness activities aimed at promoting physical and mental wellbeing. The event included expert-led demonstrations and take-home practices for daily wellbeing.
