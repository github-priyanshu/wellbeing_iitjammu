About Us

Indian Institute of Technology Jammu (IIT Jammu) is an autonomous institute established in 2016 with the aim of nurturing interdisciplinary research and high-quality technical education. The Wellbeing Club is an initiative within the campus community to promote mental and emotional wellness through targeted programs, awareness campaigns and peer support. We collaborate with institute departments and student groups to run workshops, counselling sessions and campus-wide events that encourage resilience, inclusivity and healthy lifestyles. Our mission is to provide accessible support, reduce stigma around mental health and create a campus environment where every student feels supported. Activities include mindfulness workshops, stress-management seminars, peer-support groups and referral to professional counselling when required.
