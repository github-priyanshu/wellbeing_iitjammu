Welcome to the Wellbeing Club — IIT Jammu

The Wellbeing Club at IIT Jammu is dedicated to promoting mental health, resilience and a supportive campus community. We organize workshops, counselling drives, mindfulness sessions and awareness events to help students and staff maintain emotional balance and build healthy habits. Join our events to learn practical strategies for stress management, connect with peers, and access confidential support when needed.

FAQs

How can I book a counselling session?
You can email the Wellbeing Club at wellbeingclub@iitjammu.ac.in or check the 'Support' page for phone contacts and office hours. Sessions are confidential and free for students.
Are workshops open to all students?
Yes — workshops, mindfulness sessions and awareness events are open to all IIT Jammu students and staff. Some events may require prior registration.
Where is the Wellbeing Club office located?
The Wellbeing Club operates from the campus student services area. For directions and the full institute address, see the contact details in the footer.
